The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting here, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is the primary breed used used as a detectiondog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and lack of inherited health problem.
